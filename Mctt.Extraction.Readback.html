Lemma read_nf_order_sound : forall s d m,
    {{ Rnf d in s ‚Üò m }} ->
    read_nf_order s d
with read_ne_order_sound : forall s d m,
    {{ Rne d in s ‚Üò m }} ->
    read_ne_order s d
with read_typ_order_sound : forall s d m,
    {{ Rtyp d in s ‚Üò m }} ->
    read_typ_order s d.
Proof with (econstructor; intros; functional_eval_rewrite_clear; mauto).
  - clear read_nf_order_sound; induction 1...
  - clear read_ne_order_sound; induction 1...
  - clear read_typ_order_sound; induction 1...
Qed.

The definitions of read_*_impl already come with soundness proofs, so we only need to prove completeness. However, the completeness is also obvious from the soundness of eval orders and functional nature of readback.

Ltac functional_read_complete :=
  lazymatch goal with
  | |- exists (_ : ?T), _ =>
      let Horder := fresh "Horder" in
      assert T as Horder by mauto 3;

      lazymatch goal with
      | |- exists _, ?L = _ =>

          eexists; reflexivity

  end.
